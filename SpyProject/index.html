Welcome to the Spy House

In this project i am building a generic website that will seem to ask mundane questions to normal users, but you are secretly are looking for a spy! Anyone visiting your website will be asked a series of questions, only the spy will be able to give specific answers you expect. if all questions are answered correctly, you will post a secret message in console for the spy to read!


Here are the four conditions you will check for the spy:

  • The spy has the same first letter of her First Name and Last Name
  • The Spy is between the age of 20 and 30(exclusive of 20 and 30)
  • The Spy is at least 170 centimeter tall.
  • The Spy has a pet name that ends with the letter "i"
